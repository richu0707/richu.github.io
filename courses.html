Our Courses

At ST. Vincent Pallotti College of Engineering and Technology, we offer a wide range of courses to equip you with the skills necessary for your future career. Below is a list of our available courses:

Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Computer Science
This program focuses on computer science principles and includes software development, data structures, and algorithms.
Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Electronics and Communication
This course emphasizes electronics, communication systems, and circuit design, preparing students for a career in the tech industry.
Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Mechanical Engineering
Mechanical Engineering involves the design, analysis, and manufacturing of mechanical systems, integrating principles from engineering, physics, and materials science.
Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Civil Engineering
This program covers the design and construction of infrastructure projects, focusing on sustainability and innovation.
Master of Engineering (M.E.) in Computer Science
Aimed at advanced studies in computer science, this program includes topics like artificial intelligence, machine learning, and big data analytics.
Master of Engineering (M.E.) in Structural Engineering
This program focuses on designing and analyzing structures, ensuring they can withstand loads and forces.
Diploma in Engineering
A foundational program for engineering, covering essential principles and practical applications in various fields.
Ph.D. in Engineering
A research-oriented program, where students work on innovative projects, contributing to knowledge and technology in engineering.
Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Cyber Security
This course prepares students to protect systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks and security breaches.
